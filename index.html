About Me

My name is Roberto Hernandez. I'm originally from Mexico City but am currently living here in Querétaro. By profession, I am a Data Analyst for a global nutrition company, where I enjoy diving into complex datasets to uncover business insights. A good example of my work is a recent project where I developed a call volume forecast for a large call centre. This involved reviewing a detailed, three-year dataset—broken down into 30-minute intervals across multiple languages and agent skills—to accurately predict future trends. I primarily work within the Microsoft ecosystem to build practical, enterprise-level solutions. Outside of my professional life, I like to maintain a healthy balance. I'm an avid runner, I enjoy exploring my creative side through cooking, and I always have some music on in the background.
